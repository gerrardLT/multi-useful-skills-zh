# 公司研究模板

使用这个模板创建一份带高管洞察的公司画像。

## 来源
改编自 `https://github.com/deanpeters/product-manager-prompts` 仓库中的 `prompts/company-profile-executive-insights-research.md`。

## 模板
```markdown
## Executive Insights Company Profile

### Research Objective
- **Company Name:** [公司名称]
- **Research Purpose:** [为什么做这份研究]
- **Key Questions:**
  - [问题 1]
  - [问题 2]
  - [问题 3]

### Company Overview
**Basic Information:**
- **Name:** [正式名称]
- **Headquarters:** [地点]
- **Industry:** [行业]
- **Founded:** [年份]
- **Size:** [员工数/营收/融资]

**Brief History:**
- [关键里程碑]

### Executive Quotes on Strategic Vision
- **CEO:** [引述 + 来源 + 日期]
- **COO:** [引述 + 来源 + 日期]
- **VP of Product:** [引述 + 来源 + 日期]
- **Group PM:** [引述 + 来源 + 日期]

### Detailed Product Insights
- **Product Strategy Overview:** [总结]
- **Recent Launches/Innovations:**
  1. [发布 1]
  2. [发布 2]

### Transformation Strategies
- **Digital Transformation:** [总结]
- **AI Transformation:** [总结]
- **Agile Transformation:** [总结]

### Organizational Impact of Product Management
- **Role of PM in Strategic Decisions:** [总结]
- **Cross-Functional Collaboration:** [总结]
- **PM Career Paths:** [总结]

### Future Roadmap and Challenges
- **Upcoming Initiatives:** [总结]
- **Anticipated Challenges:** [总结]

### Product-Led Growth Insights
- **PLG Strategies:** [总结]
- **Data-Driven Product Decisions:** [总结]
```
